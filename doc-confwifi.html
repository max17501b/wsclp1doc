WSC-LP1 User Manual

Installation

Configuring the Radio

Before We Begin

Your WSC-LP1 has to be powered before we can configure the radio. The most convenient method is to power the unit with a 9V battery. This way you can work nearby your WIFI router where you know the signals will be good.

Or you can power the unit with a 24VAC wall transformer. In that case you'll have to work close to the wall transformer.

What You Need

  • The name and password for your wireless network
  • A wireless device such as a smartphone or notebook computer
  • Your WSC-LP1, powered either by battery or 24VAC

Cheat Sheet

Here's the short & sweet of what we're going to do:
  1. Connect your wireless device to the WSC-LP1
  2. Browse to 1.1.1.1
  3. Select your WIFI network from a list
  4. Enter your WIFI network password
  5. Test and save the configuration

Connect Your Wireless Device to the WSC-LP1

Using the WIFI functions on your wireless device, scan for a network named "WSC-LP1". Select it. No password is needed. If the WSC-LP1 network does not appear, see this troubleshooting topic.

Start a Browser and Go To 1.1.1.1

Wait for your wireless device to connect, start up a browser and go to the address "1.1.1.1". Note that the WSC-LP1 follows web standards. Internet Explorer does not. You may get unpredictable results using I.E.

Enter Your Network Name and Password

In a few seconds a page appears listing the networks within range of the WSC-LP1. If your network is in the list, select it.

If your network is not in the list, select the link "other...."

Enter your network name (if requested) and password. Press the "Test" button. If the test fails you can retry, re-enter your password or cancel. Give it a few tries before giving up.

If the test succeeds, you're good to go. Note the IP address assigned to the WSC-LP1 by your WIFI router. You may need this in the future. Then press the "Save" button.

Congratulations

You've done the hardest step setting up your WSC-LP1, by far. After saving your network credentials, the WSC-LP1 restarts and connects to your WIFI network. Your wireless device may issue an error message when the WSC-LP1 reconnects. This is normal.

Wait for your wireless device to reconnect to you network

Once your wireless device notices the WSC-LP1 controller has disappeared it will automatically reconnect to your WIFI network. You can do that manually as well.

The final page produced by the WSC-LP1 controller includes a link to the controller on your WIFI network. DO NOT click this link until your wireless device has re-connected to your WIFI network.
