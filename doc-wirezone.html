Wiring

Zone Wiring

The COM wire is connected to either COM terminal on the 8-terminal wiring block at the bottom of the controller.

Solenoid "hot" wires are connected to the terminals labeled "1" through "6".

There is no special order to the solenoid wires -- put them in any way you like. When you configure the zones later you can name and re-order the zones.
