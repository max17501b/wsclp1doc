Settings

Time Zone

Select the time zone for your controller.

In more detail, make sure the controller time zone is the same as your browser time zone. As far as I can tell, modern browsers get their time zone from their host operating system, and you cannot do much to change it. So it's probably safe to say the WSC-LP1 time zone should be the same as the host PC/whatever which runs your browser.

If the WSC-LP1 time zone is out of sync with your browser time zone things can get confusing. So try to keep them the same.
