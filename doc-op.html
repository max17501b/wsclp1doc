WSC-LP1 User Manual

Operation

The WSC-LP1 is controlled using your web browser. The controller is connected to your LAN through your WIFI router. See "Configuring the Radio" for details regarding WIFI setup.

Navigation

All control functions of the WSC-LP1 are accessed through the controller home page.

The Activities Page shows the status of all enabled zones and and watering activity. On this page your can manually turn zones on & off, manage the work queue, and view the activities log.
The Schedules Page shows the status of the scheduler and and all watering programs. In this page you can manage the scheduler and create & modify watering programs.
The Settings Page controls general controller settings. On this page you define the controller's name, time zone, manage zones and define other miscellaneous features.
The WIFI Page controls the WIFI configuration. You select the WIFI router and password used by the controller to log onto your LAN. You can also reset the WIFI configuration so you can switch to a new router.

The Status Log

Click the "Status Log" button to view the controller status log. The log may be helpful for troubleshooting.
